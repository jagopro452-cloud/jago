## 📥 INSTALLATION STEPS (Super Simple)

### Step 1️⃣: Download
- Click the link above
- Wait for download (should take 1-2 minutes)
- You'll see a progress bar

### Step 2️⃣: Open Downloads
- Open **Files** or **Downloads** app on your phone
- Look for `jago-customer-v1.0.55-release.apk` or `jago-pilot-v1.0.57-release.apk`

### Step 3️⃣: Tap to Install
- Just tap the file!
- A screen will appear asking to install

### Step 4️⃣: Allow Unknown Apps (One Time Only)
**If you see:** "Unknown apps cannot be installed"
- Tap **Settings**
- Turn **ON** "Allow from this source"
- Come back and tap the file again

**That's it!** Your phone is just being safe. This is completely normal.

### Step 5️⃣: Wait for Installation
- Let it install (takes 1-2 minutes)
- When done, you'll see **"Open"** button

### Step 6️⃣: Open App
- Tap **"Open"**
- Or find **JAGO Pro** icon on your home screen

### Step 7️⃣: Sign Up
- Enter your phone number or email
- Create password
- You're ready to go! 🎉

## 🚨 COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

### "Download won't start"
✅ Solution: Make sure you have WiFi or 4G connected  
✅ Try a different browser (Chrome works best)

### "Can't tap the file to install"
✅ Solution: You need to allow unknown apps (see Step 4 above)

### "Not enough storage"
✅ Solution: Delete some old photos/videos and try again  
✅ You need at least 150 MB free space

### "App crashes after opening"
✅ Solution: Restart your phone and try again  
✅ Make sure you have latest Android version

### "Can't sign up"
✅ Make sure you have internet (WiFi or 4G)  
✅ Check that phone number is correct
