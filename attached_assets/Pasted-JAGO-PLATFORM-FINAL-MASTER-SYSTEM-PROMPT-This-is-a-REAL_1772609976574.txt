HYBRID REVENUE MODEL

Every service must support two modes:

Subscription Model
Commission Model

Admin must be able to toggle the mode.

Subscription Model:

Driver must purchase subscription before going online.

Examples:

Daily Plan ₹60
Weekly Plan ₹300
Monthly Plan ₹999

Commission Model:

Driver pays percentage commission per ride.

Example:

Ride Fare ₹100

Commission 15% = ₹15
GST 18% = ₹2.70
Insurance = ₹2

Total deduction = ₹19.70

Driver earnings = Fare minus deduction.
